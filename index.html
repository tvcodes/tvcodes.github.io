The person behind the blog

About Me

Hi, I'm the person behind Me-niverse — a small corner of the internet where movies, music, and books collide. I started this blog because every story I fall in love with feels like it deserves its own galaxy: a little universe where it can be remembered, picked apart, and shared.

When I'm not writing, you'll probably find me chasing a plot twist I didn't see coming, building a playlist for a mood I can't quite name, or getting lost in a book that refuses to let go.

This isn't a portfolio so much as a logbook — a record of every thought that felt big enough to deserve its own orbit.
